Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Contribution graph

A GitHub-style heatmap that captures your daily AI usage patterns at a glance. See your work unfold over time.

Privacy-first

All data stored locally in your browser. No servers, no analytics, no accounts. Your usage data never leaves your machine.

Zero configuration

Install once, forget about it. Automatically detects when you send prompts on supported AI platforms.

Streak tracking

Current and longest streaks keep you motivated. See consecutive days of AI-assisted coding at a glance.

Daily & weekly stats

Granular breakdowns by platform, day and week. Understand which tools drive your productivity.

Multi-platform

Unified tracking across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and DeepSeek from a single extension popup.

How it works

Three steps. That's it.

1

Install the extension

Add ContriAI to Chrome. No accounts, no sign-ups, no permissions beyond what's needed.

2

Use AI as you normally do

Chat with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini or DeepSeek. Every prompt you send is automatically tracked locally.

3

See your contributions

Open the extension popup to see your contribution graph, streak stats and platform breakdown — instantly.
